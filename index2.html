The Methodology

Two metrics that quantify your market exposure.

Built on labor economics data and AI adoption timelines—not generic job titles. This is the exact analytical framework we use to structure your Executive Audit.

Score One

Displacement Risk
Index™ (DRI)

Measures how exposed your job tasks are to AI — right now, not in five years. Scored 0–100. A high score means parts of your role are already being automated in companies like yours.

  • Task repetitiveness and rules-based execution
  • Current AI capability maturity in your specific sector
  • Pace of enterprise AI adoption within your industry
  • Proportion of daily output replicable by large language models
  • Seniority level and ratio of strategic oversight to pure execution
  • US regulatory environment and compliance moats
Low → Critical Risk
Score Two

Pivot Readiness
Score™ (PRS)

Measures how much of what you’ve already built — your skills, your experience, your industry knowledge — transfers into roles that are gaining value as AI takes over routine work. Scored 0–100. A high score means you have more options than you think.

  • Transferability of domain expertise to AI strategy or governance
  • Baseline analytical and complex problem-solving capabilities
  • Years of experience (functioning as a judgment anchor)
  • Niche expertise and high-level stakeholder management
  • Human-centric skills: complex negotiation, ethics, alignment
  • Proximity to high-growth, automation-resistant sectors
Limited → High Leverage

Frequently Asked Questions.

Is the free score actually useful, or just a gimmick?

It’s a real number — not a lead magnet dressed up as analysis. Your free DRI score is calculated from your actual profile and benchmarked against current sector data. It won’t tell you what to do next (that’s the audit), but it will tell you clearly whether you have a problem worth solving.

Why is the Executive Audit a $995 investment?

You aren't paying for an automated printout; you are paying for a deeply researched strategy tailored to protect a six-figure earning potential. A bad career decision — a pivot that doesn’t work — can cost you $30,000 to $100,000 in lost income.

Will this report just make me more anxious about the future?

Actually, the goal is the exact opposite. Anxiety usually comes from the unknown. By seeing exactly which parts of your job are vulnerable and—more importantly—which of your skills are "AI-proof," you go from worrying about something vague to having a specific plan you can act on.

What happens to my resume after I upload it?

Your resume is used to generate your report and nothing else. It is not stored after delivery, not used to train any AI model, and never shared with third parties. We don’t monetize your data.

I’m in a very specialized, senior role. Can you still analyze it?

Yes. That’s exactly who the Executive Audit is for. We don't just look at "what" you do, but "how" you do it. We factor in things like complex negotiations, strategic oversight, and relationship management—areas where you hold a massive edge over software.

Is there a human involved in this process?

For the $995 Executive Audit, yes. While we leverage massive datasets from labor economists to do the heavy lifting, every single premium report is reviewed by a real person before delivery. We make sure the pivot pathways actually make logical and financial sense for a human being, not just a line of code.
