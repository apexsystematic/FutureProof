§ 2 What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to deliver your report. We do not collect data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond fulfilling your order.

  • Contact information — your name and email address, provided when you submit the order form
  • CV / Résumé — the professional document you submit as part of the intake process. This is the primary input used to generate your report
  • Intake questionnaire responses — your answers about your role, industry, goals, and career context
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our third-party payment processor. We never see or store your card details
  • Communication records — emails exchanged between you and FutureProof regarding your order

We do not use tracking pixels, behavioural analytics tools, or any form of automated profiling beyond what is needed to generate your report.

§ 3 Why We Collect It — Legal Basis

Every piece of data we collect has a specific purpose and a lawful basis under GDPR:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — your name, email, CV, and questionnaire responses are processed to fulfil your order and deliver your report. Without this data, we cannot provide the service.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) — we may retain certain transaction records to comply with Spanish tax law and accounting obligations (Agencia Tributaria requirements).
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — we retain email correspondence for a reasonable period to handle disputes, refund requests, or revision requests related to your order.

We do not process your data based on consent or for direct marketing. We will never send you unsolicited marketing emails.

§ 4 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • CV and questionnaire data — deleted within 30 days of your report being delivered
  • Email address and name — retained for up to 12 months after order completion to handle any follow-up queries, revisions, or disputes, then deleted
  • Transaction records — retained for 5 years in accordance with Spanish accounting and tax law (Ley General Tributaria)
  • Email correspondence — retained for 12 months after the last interaction, then deleted
Your CV is not stored beyond 30 days We do not build a database of client CVs. Your CV is used solely to produce your report and is permanently deleted within 30 days of delivery. We do not use your CV to train AI models or for any purpose other than fulfilling your order.

§ 5 Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only where strictly necessary to deliver the service:

  • Payment processors — your payment is handled by a third-party processor (e.g. Stripe or equivalent). They receive your billing details but not your CV or report content. They operate under their own privacy policies and are GDPR-compliant.
  • Email service providers — we use a third-party email platform to send you your report and order communications. Your email address is shared with this provider solely for this purpose.
  • AI infrastructure providers — your CV and questionnaire data is processed through AI systems to generate your report. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and may not use your data for any other purpose.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, recruitment agencies, or any third party for commercial purposes.

To analyze your career data and generate our intelligence reports, we utilize advanced third-party service providers (such as AI models, cloud hosting platforms, and payment processors). Some of these providers may process data on servers located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), primarily in the United States.

These international data transfers are strictly executed under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, by utilizing Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or via Adequacy decisions, ensuring your data is treated with the rigorous protections mandated by the GDPR.
