Proprietary Methodology

Two Scores That Change
How You See Your Career.

Grounded in labour economics research, AI adoption data, and actual task-level analysis — not a form that asks for your job title and calls it a day.

Score One

Displacement Risk
Index™ (DRI)

Measures how exposed your role is to automation — not just whether it could be replaced, but how quickly tasks are already starting to shift. Scored 0–100, where higher means act sooner

  • Task repetitiveness & rules-based content in your daily work
  • Current AI capability maturity in your specific sector
  • Rate of AI adoption in your industry (exploratory vs. standardised)
  • Proportion of output already replicable by generative AI tools
  • Seniority level and supervisory vs. execution responsibilities
  • Your local market's AI maturity and regulatory environment
Low → Critical
Score Two

Pivot Readiness
Score™ (PRS)

Measures how much of your existing background lines up with roles that are holding their own against automation. Scored 0–100 — the higher the score, the more options you have.

  • Transferability of domain expertise to AI strategy or governance roles
  • Existing analytical, technical, or data-adjacent skills
  • Years of experience and seniority (judgment anchor value)
  • Niche expertise, local market knowledge, or language advantages
  • Human-centric skills: negotiation, ethics, relationship management
  • Educational background and proximity to AI-relevant disciplines
Limited → Excellent
