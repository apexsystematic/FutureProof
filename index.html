The Methodology

Two metrics that quantify
your market exposure.

Built on labor economics data and AI adoption timelines—not generic job titles. This is the exact analytical framework we use to structure your Executive Audit.

Score One

Displacement Risk
Index™ (DRI)

Measures your immediate vulnerability to AI automation. Scored 0–100. Higher scores indicate tasks that are already facing severe margin compression or direct elimination in the current market.

  • Task repetitiveness and rules-based execution
  • Current AI capability maturity in your specific sector
  • Pace of enterprise AI adoption within your industry
  • Proportion of daily output replicable by large language models
  • Seniority level and ratio of strategic oversight to pure execution
  • US regulatory environment and compliance moats
Low → Critical Risk
Score Two

Pivot Readiness
Score™ (PRS)

Measures your leverage. Scored 0–100. A high score means your existing background directly translates into high-value roles that hold their market premium as automation increases.

  • Transferability of domain expertise to AI strategy or governance
  • Baseline analytical and complex problem-solving capabilities
  • Years of experience (functioning as a judgment anchor)
  • Niche expertise and high-level stakeholder management
  • Human-centric skills: complex negotiation, ethics, alignment
  • Proximity to high-growth, automation-resistant sectors
Limited → High Leverage

Is the free score actually useful, or just a gimmick?

It's highly useful. The free baseline score takes your immediate data and plots it against current industry automation metrics. It won't give you a step-by-step plan to fix your career, but it will clearly tell you if you need one.

Why is the Executive Audit a $995 investment?

Because it's an investment in risk management for your livelihood. You aren't paying for an automated printout; you are paying for a deeply researched strategy tailored to protect a six-figure earning potential. It costs less than a single bad career move, but provides the exact roadmap to stay ahead of market shifts.

Will this report just make me more anxious about the future?

Actually, the goal is the exact opposite. Anxiety usually comes from the unknown. By seeing exactly which parts of your job are vulnerable and—more importantly—which of your skills are "AI-proof," you move from a state of worry to a state of execution.

What happens to my resume after I upload it?

We handle your data the way we'd want ours handled. Your resume is used solely to generate your assessment. Once your report is delivered, your data is securely deleted from our active systems. We maintain strict privacy compliance.

I’m in a very specialized, senior role. Can you still analyze it?

Yes. That’s exactly who the Executive Audit is for. We don't just look at "what" you do, but "how" you do it. We factor in things like complex negotiations, strategic oversight, and relationship management—areas where you hold a massive edge over software.

Is there a human involved in this process?

For the $995 Executive Audit and B2B packages, yes. While we leverage massive datasets from labor economists to do the heavy lifting, every single premium report is reviewed by a real person before delivery. We make sure the pivot pathways actually make logical and financial sense for a human being, not just a line of code.
